## Data Quality Checks

1. **Data format is either:**
    1. tabular with csv extension
    1. direct link to shapefile
1. **Tabular delimiter is one of:**
    1. COMMA [ , ]
    1. SEMI-COLON [ ; ]
    1. PIPE [ \| ]
    1. PLUS [ + ]
1. **Text qualifiers if present, are double quotes [ " ]**
1. **Data is de-normalized ("deep" not "wide")**
